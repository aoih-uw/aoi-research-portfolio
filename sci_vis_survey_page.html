Scientific Visuals Interview Results

Thank you for participating in our survey about scientific visuals. We truly appreicate your time!

Who Participated?

29 researchers took part, including individuals from academia and industry. Roles included PhD students, postdocs, faculty, and professional researchers.

What Did We Ask?

The survey included questions that try to better understand how researchers' view the importance of scientific visuals, what tools they use for visualization, and common obstacles in creating visuals.

How to Read the Charts
